## How Velocity Scoring Works

Everything above is derived from velocity data, not price data. Here's the core methodology:

**Velocity score = a weighted composite of:**
- Trade frequency (how often a token is being traded, not how much)
- Unique wallet interactions (new addresses vs repeat addresses)
- Social surface area (mentions, engagement velocity on X and Discord)
- Volume trend direction (accelerating or decelerating)

**Signal tiers:**
- **SURGE** — velocity in the top 10% of recent history, accelerating
- **RISING** — above baseline, trending upward
- **STABLE** — at or near baseline, sideways
- **FADING** — below baseline, decelerating

The signal is a leading indicator of market attention, not a lagging confirmation of price movement. By the time a token shows up on price charts, the velocity signal often peaked hours or days earlier.
