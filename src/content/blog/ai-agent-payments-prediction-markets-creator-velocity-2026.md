## FAQ

### What is token velocity and how does BaseRadar measure it?

Token velocity measures how frequently a token changes hands relative to its circulating supply. BaseRadar scores velocity from 0 to 100 and assigns directional signals — SURGE, RISING, STABLE, and FALLING — based on the rate of change. Higher velocity with an upward signal historically precedes volume expansion and price movement by 24 to 72 hours. The full scoring framework is detailed on the [methodology page](/methodology).
